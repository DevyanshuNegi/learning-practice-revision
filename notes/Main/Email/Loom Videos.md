

### 4. The 2-Minute Script Structure

Your Loom videos must be heavily rehearsed, punchy, and structured like a movie trailer, not a college lecture.

- **0:00 - 0:15 (The Hook):** Face to camera, state their name, and immediately validate why you are in their inbox.
    
- **0:15 - 1:15 (The Value / Screen Share):** Shrink your face to the bubble. Show their website, show their broken DNS records, or show your 10/10 Postmaster dashboard. Explain the _cost_ of their problem.
    
- **1:15 - 1:45 (The Solution):** Explain exactly how your infrastructure or software solves this pain point.
    
- **1:45 - 2:00 (The "Soft" Call to Action):** Do not aggressively push a calendar link. Say: _"If this infrastructure makes sense for your Q3 goals, reply to this email or grab 10 minutes on my calendar below just to align on the technicals."_
    

You have the technical skills. You have the delivery engine. Now you have a mechanism (async video) that bypasses your weakness in live sales.
