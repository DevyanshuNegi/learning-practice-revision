


Modulation is the process of changing properties of a high-frequency carrier signal based on a low-frequency message signal.

### Common Parameters:

- **Carrier Signal**: High-frequency signal that carries the message
- **Modulating Signal**: The actual message/information (0 Hz, 2 Hz, 10 Hz, 50 Hz, 100 Hz in this case)
- **Modulated Signal**: The resulting signal after modulation
